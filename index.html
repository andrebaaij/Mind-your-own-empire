About

You are a Mind, landed on a new planet. You command/upgrade your minions/buildings. They will be mining resources, fighting off the original inhabitants/other interested races and building for you... The story will be a bit better in the future :)


Alpha version

Currently the game is under heavy development as a sideproject. The game is a procedurally sandboxed rts, you could say it is kind of a minecraft rts in that aspect. Just give it a try and let us know what you think at @andrebaaij


Please note, performance on older hardware is not yet great, we hope to improve this a lot. We are pushing the limits for a browser based game, so please be patient.


If you are interested in looking at the code, open a bug or help us squash those bugs, look at our github repository.
